**Owner:** [Information Security Manager]  
**Classification:** Internal

## 1. Purpose

This policy defines the information security principles, requirements, and responsibilities for protecting [Organization Name]'s information assets.

## 2. Scope

This policy applies to all employees, contractors, consultants, and third parties who access organizational information or systems.

## 3. Policy Statements

### 3.1 Access Control

- Access to information shall be granted on a need-to-know basis
- All access shall be authorized, documented, and reviewed regularly
- Privileged access shall be restricted and monitored
- User access shall be revoked upon termination or role change

### 3.2 Data Classification

Information shall be classified according to the following levels:

| Classification | Description | Handling Requirements |
|---------------|-------------|----------------------|
| **Confidential** | Highly sensitive business information | Encrypted, restricted access |
| **Internal** | Internal business information | Access control, no public sharing |
| **Public** | Information approved for public release | No restrictions |

### 3.3 Acceptable Use

- Information systems shall be used for authorized business purposes
- Personal use shall be limited and not interfere with business operations
- Users shall not attempt to bypass security controls
- Users shall not install unauthorized software

### 3.4 Password and Authentication

- Passwords shall meet minimum complexity requirements
- Multi-factor authentication shall be used for sensitive systems
- Passwords shall not be shared or stored insecurely
- Authentication credentials shall be changed regularly

### 3.5 Data Protection

- Sensitive data shall be encrypted in transit and at rest
- Data shall be backed up regularly and tested for recovery
- Data retention periods shall be defined and enforced
- Data disposal shall follow secure destruction procedures

### 3.6 Network Security

- Network traffic shall be monitored for threats
- Firewalls shall protect network boundaries
- Wireless networks shall use strong encryption
- Remote access shall use secure VPN connections

### 3.7 Physical Security

- Access to facilities shall be controlled
- Visitors shall be escorted in secure areas
- Equipment shall be protected from environmental threats
- Clean desk policy shall be enforced

### 3.8 Incident Response

- Security incidents shall be reported immediately
- Incidents shall be investigated and documented
- Lessons learned shall be incorporated into improvements
- See: `procedures/incident-response.md`

## 4. Compliance

### 4.1 Monitoring

Information security compliance shall be monitored through:
- Security assessments
- Internal audits
- Penetration testing
- Log reviews

### 4.2 Violations

Violations of this policy may result in:
- Disciplinary action up to and including termination
- Legal action where applicable
- Revocation of access privileges

## 5. Exceptions

Exceptions to this policy require:
- Written justification
- Risk assessment
- Approval from the Information Security Manager
- Time-limited duration with review

## 6. Review

This policy shall be reviewed annually or when significant changes occur.
