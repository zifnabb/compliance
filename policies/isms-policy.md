## 1. Purpose

This policy establishes the framework for [Organization Name]'s Information Security Management System (ISMS) in accordance with ISO/IEC 27001:2022.

## 2. Scope

This ISMS applies to:
- All information assets owned or managed by [Organization Name]
- All employees, contractors, and third parties with access to organizational information
- All locations where information is processed, stored, or transmitted
- All systems and processes within the defined ISMS boundary

## 3. Information Security Objectives

The organization commits to:

1. **Confidentiality** - Protect information from unauthorized disclosure
2. **Integrity** - Ensure accuracy and completeness of information
3. **Availability** - Ensure authorized access to information when required

### 3.1 Measurable Objectives

| Objective | Target | Measurement Method |
|-----------|--------|-------------------|
| Security incidents | < [X] per quarter | Incident tracking system |
| Security awareness training | 100% completion | Training records |
| Vulnerability remediation | < [X] days critical | Vulnerability scanner |

## 4. Leadership Commitment

Top management demonstrates commitment to the ISMS by:
- Ensuring the ISMS achieves its intended outcomes
- Providing adequate resources for ISMS implementation
- Communicating the importance of information security
- Promoting continual improvement

## 5. Roles and Responsibilities

### 5.1 Top Management
- Approve ISMS policies and objectives
- Allocate resources
- Conduct management reviews

### 5.2 Information Security Manager
- Maintain the ISMS
- Coordinate security activities
- Report ISMS performance

### 5.3 Asset Owners
- Classify information assets
- Implement controls
- Manage access rights

### 5.4 All Personnel
- Comply with security policies
- Report security incidents
- Complete security training

## 6. Risk Management

The organization shall:
- Identify information security risks
- Assess risk likelihood and impact
- Treat risks according to the risk treatment plan
- Monitor and review risks regularly

See: `risk-management-policy.md`

## 7. Continual Improvement

The ISMS shall be continually improved through:
- Internal audits
- Management reviews
- Corrective actions
- Performance monitoring
